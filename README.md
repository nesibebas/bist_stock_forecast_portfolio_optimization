## Project Purpose

To create and forecast a portfolio of three Borsa Istanbul (BIST) stocks Turkish Airlines, Akbank, and Ar√ßelik using time series modeling (ARIMA) and volatility modeling (GARCH).

## üìâ Results and Discussion (Forecast & Performance)

### Initial Forecast Performance

* **Short-Run Success:** The 90-day price forecasts generated by the **ARIMA** model revealed good accuracy **in the short run (until mid-January)**.
  
* **Medium/Long-Run Failure:** The model's predictive capability **significantly failed in the medium and long run** (especially after March), proving incapable of predicting sudden, sharp price changes.Therefore, the importance of GARCH analysis is shown.
  
* **Observations:**  
  * THYAO and ARCLK displayed higher volatility, while AKBNK showed stability.

  * ARIMA models tended to overestimate future returns (trend continuation bias).

  * Combining ARIMA + GARCH gives more realistic forecasts by accounting for volatility persistence.

  * The actual portfolio value fell by about 15% after three months, revealing model optimism.

### Portfolio Simulation Result

* **Initial Scenario:** The simulated portfolio value largely coincided with the forecast initially.
* **Final Scenario:** Deviations began in mid-February, and the portfolio value started to decline significantly in April. By the end of May, the portfolio value had **decreased by approximately 15% to TL 850,000.**

## üí° Analytical Implications and Future Work

These results provide a critical insight: financial time series are highly sensitive not only to the underlying trend but also to volatility and exogenous shocks.

### Methodological Development Proposal:
